Rounds

Round 1:

Quiz ‚Äì Nova: It will be a quiz based round covering basic questions from general knowledge, basic aptitude questions, memes from the internet (insta etc), slight cosmos-based questions etc. Will be conducted on Quiz.com.

Round 2:

PDF Paradox: It is a three-PDF challenge where participants must unlock each PDF (referred to as an "orbit") by solving the tasks provided within it. The password for each subsequent PDF is the answer to the previous one, requiring participants to solve the PDFs sequentially to progress to the next round. The round is time-bound. The tasks or questions in each PDF are paragraph-based, and the answers are single words. Participants must think critically and solve the challenges.

Round 3:

Celestial Hunt (Treasure Hunt): It's a time-bound round where tasks will be assigned to each team. After solving the tasks, teams will receive chits containing riddles related to locations inside our college campus. It's a total treasure hunt based round. Remaining details will be given on event day.

Round 4:

Surprise round: This round is a surprise for all participants. Competitors will compete individually. A Winner Team and the first runner-up Team will be selected from the participants.
